Welcome to IT3 Consultants

Specialists in Unix, Open Source, Disaster Recovery & Enterprise IT Solutions

We deliver expert technology services across Unix/Linux environments, disaster recovery, Open Source projects, and enterprise-grade Internet and Intranet infrastructure. Our team helps organisations build secure, optimised systems for long-term success.

Why "IT3"?

Our name reflects the three pillars of our approach to Information Technology:

🖥️ Information Technology

We offer deep expertise in system architecture, infrastructure management, and performance optimisation — especially in Unix and Linux environments, enterprise systems, and mission-critical applications.

🔗 Interface Technology

We create intuitive, secure, and high-performance user experiences that bridge systems, applications, and users with ease.

🔧 Integration Technology

We specialise in bringing diverse technologies together to work in harmony — improving efficiency, reliability, and ROI across your IT landscape.

What We Do

🌐 Open Source Innovation

We actively contribute to and leverage Open Source projects — delivering flexible, cost-effective, and secure solutions. 🔒 Disaster Recovery Planning

Our comprehensive disaster recovery services help protect your operations and data from disruptions, ensuring business continuity. We develop, maintain, and support Relax-and-Recover (ReaR) — the de facto standard bare metal disaster recovery framework on Linux, used by enterprises worldwide including Red Hat and Oracle environments.
